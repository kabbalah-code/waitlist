## üéØ ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY BY CHANNEL TYPE

### Twitter/X Strategy

**For Micro-Influencers (FREE):**
1. Follow and engage with their content (retweets, replies)
2. Send DM: "Love your content on [topic]. We're building KABBALAH CODE - [1 sentence pitch]. Would you be interested in collaborating?"
3. Offer: Early access, free NFT, revenue share, guest post opportunity
4. Follow up after 1 week if no response

**For Mid-Tier Influencers:**
1. Engage with content for 2-3 weeks
2. Send professional DM with media kit
3. Propose specific collaboration (guest post, interview, AMA)
4. Offer: Payment, NFT, revenue share, or cross-promotion

**For Mega Influencers:**
1. Use agency/PR firm
2. Prepare professional pitch deck
3. Offer significant payment or partnership
4. Expect 2-4 week response time

### Telegram Strategy

**For Communities (FREE):**
1. Join community
2. Introduce yourself professionally
3. Share valuable content (not spam)
4. Ask for feedback on your project
5. Offer: Early access, NFT drops, community rewards
6. Post updates 1-2x per week (not more)

**For Investment Funds:**
1. Join channel
2. Share project updates
3. Engage with fund's content
4. Send DM with pitch deck
